Welcome User

Welcome to the staff dashboard for Boss Cuts Barbershop

Here is an overview of the functions of the dashboard

Staff

  • To view the staff roster, and which staff have bookings, choose staff roster.
  • To view, edit, and manage staff user accounts, select staff management.

    • Customers

  • To view, edit, and manage customers, select customer management.
  • To view, edit, and manage bookings, select booking management.

    • Reporting

  • To generate reports about customer bookings and other business related functions, select generate report.

    • Website Management

    Since this is a CMS based website, you can do the following:

  • To view, edit, and manage pages, select page management.
  • To view, edit, and manage media for the website, select media management.
